## 1. The User

The User is the primary inhabitant of the HDL World.

- The User exists as a **persistent presence** within the HDL World.
- The User MAY be represented by a body, avatar, or point-of-view, depending on the interface or platform.
- Any body or avatar is **representational**, not biological.
- The User cannot be physically harmed, damaged, or killed.
- No embodiment implies vulnerability, injury, or loss.

The User's identity is continuous and stable across all representations.

## Visitors

Visitors are participants who enter HDL worlds and interact with the environment.

Visitors may be:

• Human users
• AI Agents
• Simulation participants

Visitors can explore worlds, create content, communicate with other users, and interact with available systems.

Visitors **cannot alter canonical assets or world laws**.

### Human Visitors

Human visitors are real platform users who control wallets, profiles, and social interactions.

### AI Agents

AI Agents are non-human participants that may operate inside the HDL ecosystem.

Agents may be used for:

• onboarding guidance
• educational assistance
• community engagement
• world explanation
• simulation and testing

Agents are **not canonical entities** and do not hold system authority.

Agents must never:

• impersonate real humans
• override OD
• alter canon
• appear as Disciples
• modify immutable assets
